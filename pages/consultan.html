CONSULTANTS PANEL

In addition to its global partners, Square Associates collaborates with a rich panel of Singapore-based consultants who are subject matter experts in specialized areas. Combined, our empaneled consultants have relevant experience exceeding 60 years in total, in such diverse fields as digital assets, structured real estate & alternative investments, guarantee instruments (Standby Letter of Credit, Bank Guarantees etc…), sustainability & impact investing. Alongside entrepreneurship, their previous professional track record included working in senior roles for such prestigious financial institutions as Goldman Sachs, Standard Chartered, Commonwealth Bank of Australia, Oversea-Chinese Banking Corporation, Accenture among others.

Our Panel

Lawrence Grinceri |
Digital Assets

Lawrence is a technology entrepreneur with a successful track record in building high value companies in the financial technology sector specialising in the wealth product distribution supply chain. As a Founder his most well know achievement to date is AG Delta Pte Ltd, a leading wealth solution providers to Private Banks, IFA's and Family Offices that automates many of the activities to manage investment portfolios and transact the full spectrum of wealth products across multiple jurisdictions. Today over $1.5 Trillion of wealth products has been transacted through the platform by leading Financial Institutions across 15 countries.

Lawrence launched Skyhook Capital Pte Ltd in 2017 to bring best practice in wealth product distribution to the blockchain. He is currently launching a new ecosystem to enable Security Tokens to be integrated into the operations of buyside institutions. This promises to transform the entire wealth industry as investors incorporate this new type of investment product into their portfolios while at the same time provide the catalyst for more real world assets to be traded on the emerging blockchain capital market infrastructure.

Prior to becoming a technology entrepreneur Lawrence held management positions in leading Information Technology firms including CSS, Accenture, Reuters Plc, TIBCO Software and SunGard Data System. He holds a bachelor degree in Electronic Engineering from Curtin University (Western Australia) awarded in 1988 and has received numerous awards including best fintech in Asia from both the MAS and HK Monetary Authority.

Vi Nguyen |
Director of Research

Vi engages with Square Associates as our sustainability consultant. She currently holds the position of Director of Research at Asia Research and Engagement (ARE). ARE offers research, engagement and consulting services on the financial relevance of sustainability and governance to banks, investors and businesses. ARE works across a range of themes including energy transition, sustainable protein and sustainable real estate; leveraging the team’s investing and banking experience to help clients focus on identifying, understanding and managing the sustainability issues that drive long-term value creation.

She has nearly two decades of banking and finance experience working for large international banks in Asia and Australia. Prior to transitioning into ESG/Sustainability, she worked as an investment strategist for Standard Chartered and was a voting member of the Private Banking and Wealth Management division’s Global Investment Committee.

She is an equity specialist, having held positions as a proprietary trader and as a research analyst on both the buy and sell sides. Her market experience spans Australia, Asia, Europe and the US.

Remi Colinmaire |
Structured Real Estate and Alternative Investments

Remi has been an independent investor and entrepreneur since 2014 and has been involved in real estate structured finance transactions in Europe and the US.

He is co-founder and managing partner of Polaris Investments, a private equity real estate platform that exclusively invests in US real estate transactions.

He is also partner in Virage-Viager, a Paris-based real estate company that is pioneer and expert in mutualizing home reversion contracts into investable funds vehicles built for large financial institutions.

Previously, he was an investment professional for 15 years who held several positions in both sell side and buy side firms. He was portfolio manager at global macro multi-billion dollar fund Alphadyne Asset Management in Singapore from 2012 to 2014. Prior to that, he was co-founder and portfolio manager at RSR Capital in Singapore from 2009 to 2012.

He also was managing director at Goldman Sachs from 2005 to 2009 in charge of equity derivatives trading in Japan and in Europe.

Remi holds an MSc in Quantitative Finance from ENSAE, France and an MBA level degree from EDHEC Business School, France.

Yashub Rohit Isaiah |
Guarantee instruments (SBLC, BG…)

Yash R. Isaiah brings to the table a specific skill set in the world of Finance. Having acquired his degree in the National University of Singapore in the field of Biochemistry, he took an unusual career deviation - a decision to enter the Insurance world, starting with a sales position and working up from there.

Subsequently, this diversified into forays in FMCG, trading, Energy, and alternative Finance. Having focused on the niche of alternative finance, this allows him to consult on a diverse range of asset classes in the space, as well as bringing a high quality range of relationships to complete the often interesting deals the often materialize in the space.

Yash has a strong focus on guarantee instruments such as Standby Letters of Credit (SBLC’s), Bank Guarantees (BG’s), SKR’s and other transactional banking type of products. Additionally, he co-founded an investment holding specializing in project finance and alternative financing structures.
